Where We Are

Blue Crab Crossing is located in Crisfield, Maryland. Crisfield used to be one of the most prosperous cities in Maryland, bringing in supplies by train and hauling seafood out by train. The traintracks have long since been removed, but the watermen and their industry have not.

"The waterfront community of Crisfield, MD is the southernmost town in Maryland. Located on the Eastern Shore of the Chesapeake Bay, Crisfield is famous for; its seafood, the Maryland blue crab, its watermen, wildlife, natural beauty, simple lifestyle, and strong sense of community built on faith and hard work."

Modified Quote From www.cityofcrisfield-md.gov




About Us

We've been in business since 2008, highlighting talented local artists. We provide a place for local artists to sell their artwork and other items that are indicitive of the local area. We are not a country store, we are a nautical store. We try to carry a wide variety of nautical gift items. We sell items such as nauticle themed; kitchen ware, table linens, pewter ware, pillows, and tv trays. We carry; beachbags, beach towels, small purses, hats, and t-shirts. We even have something for your baby such as; bibs, sweaters, and decorations.
